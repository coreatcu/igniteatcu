The Weekend

Join us on November 7 and 8 at Ignite@CU, the collegiate hub for entrepreneurship.

RSVP Now

Network

Meet your peers involved in entrepreneurship at some of the nation’s most prestigious schools! We’ll be hosting breakout sessions, idea labs, and networking sessions with recruiters from exciting startups from across the country.

Learn

Each participating entrepreneurship club will be organizing a panel or workshop related to a specific field or theme of entrepreneurship. We want to build a conference where everyone can learn and contribute, and this is a fantastic opportunity to make new connections with both counterpart clubs and the speakers they bring to Ignite.

Create

Whether you’re looking for a co-founder or already leading an exciting startup, we have programming that fits the entire spectrum. Our idea labs provide the environment for new connections to be made among undergrads looking to start companies, while our expo allows up-and-coming ventures to display their work and attract new talent and interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

We’re still planning a lot for the weekend! Feel free to reach out to us with any questions or comments.

So what is Ignite@CU?

The second annual Ignite@CU is an intercollegiate entrepreneurship conference taking place on November 7th and 8th at Columbia University. Ignite is an opportunity for the nation’s top collegiate entrepreneurial talent to come together for a fantastic weekend of learning, building, and connecting.

Who can attend?

Anyone currently enrolled in an undergraduate institution! If your college's entrepreneurship club isn't participating, we'd still love to have you! Better yet, point us to the right people to get your club involved!

Can you arrange overnight accomodations?

Yes! Tell us when you register, and we’ll pair you with a Columbia student for an overnight stay. Don’t worry, we’re super friendly. All you’ll need is a sleeping bag, toiletries, and a change of clothes!

How can I demo?

Let us know you want to demo when you register and we'll give you a slot! Easy as that.

Who's running this?

Ignite@CU is brought to you by the Columbia Organization of Rising Entrepreneurs (CORE). We’re one of the largest student groups on campus - and the only one solely dedicated to entrepreneurship. We’re proud to present our first nationwide conference!

Who should I contact for more information?

Feel free to email us, and we’ll answer as soon as possible!
